Who's Dina?

Dina has many years of first-hand experience as a front-liner servicing big companies' clients, more recently at a major US financial institution. She uses her strong communications skills, her great attention for details, investigative and problem-solving skills daily.

After many years, of just merely scratching the surface of customers' data, before passing on issues for research to the 'back office' or up the hierarchy, she wished she could dive deeper, and be a 'behind-the-scene' person who could provide insights to a company's decision-making driven by data. After a few years of soul-searching in between educating her own children, managing her household and holding a full-time job, she decided to pivot her career to data analysis. In 2021, she started her journey by learning Excel as a data analytics tool through Coursera.

Currently, Dina is pursuing a Data Analyst Diploma with OpenClassroom. She is tasked to finish 9 data analysis projects by January 2025, under the guidance of an experienced data analyst as a mentor to her.

Combining Servicing Experience
with Data Analytics

Through her diligence, she now possess skills in data wrangling using Excel, querying database using MySQL,visualization and dashboarding in both Excel and Tableau, and data analytics in Python language (using Jupyter Notebooks). As the data technology is evolving, she is continuously learning new things. Next, on her list is to learn R programming language for data analytics.

The projects in this portfolio website are hands-on projects that Dina had performed in the data analyst courses she had taken, as well as a ones that she had put together on her own.
